The Challenge

Why this program exists

In Tanzania, many children under 8 lack access to stimulating, safe, and supportive environments during their most critical years. Poverty, limited caregiver knowledge, and underfunded public services create gaps that have lifelong consequences on cognitive, social, and physical development.

WeCare Foundation works directly with families in Mbeya and Mara — training caregivers, supporting community-run spaces, and connecting families to services that give every child the foundation they deserve.

Who We Serve

Target Communities

Our program prioritizes low-income families in peri-urban and rural areas of Mbeya and Mara. We work through existing community structures — markets, schools, local government, and religious institutions — to reach the most vulnerable children.
