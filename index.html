Who We Are

Every child deserves the best start in life

Our mission is to contribute towards attainment of quality early childhood development, learning, and health outcomes through innovative solutions, community and family engagement.

Our vision is to provide access to the best start of life for all children in early development, learning, and health across Tanzania.
